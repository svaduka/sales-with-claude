# Insurance Domain Knowledge

## Overview
Understanding insurance operations, terminology, and workflows is essential to design solutions that fit business processes. Current capability gap is MEDIUM - finance/ERP background but not insurance-specific.

**Why Learn:** Credibility in proposal depends on demonstrating understanding of underwriting, claims, policy administration; enables better solution design aligned with insurance workflows.

---

## Topics

### Topic 1: Insurance Fundamentals & Terminology
**Why Learn:** Must speak the client's language; understand basic insurance concepts used in RFP

**Learning Objectives:**
- Learn insurance types: Property & Casualty (P&C), Life, Health, Specialty Lines
- Master key terms: premium, deductible, policy, claim, underwriting, loss ratio, combined ratio, reserves
- Understand insurance value chain: distribution → underwriting → policy admin → claims → reinsurance
- Learn Greek insurance market structure and regulations

**Resources:**
- **Course:** "Introduction to Insurance" (The Institutes/Coursera)
- **Book:** "Principles of Risk Management and Insurance" (George Rejda)
- **Website:** Hellenic Association of Insurance Companies (EAEE) - Greek insurance market
- **Guide:** Insurance terminology glossary

**Practice/Labs:**
- Create insurance terminology flashcards (50 terms)
- Read sample insurance policies (auto, property, liability)
- Map Ethniki's lines of business to insurance concepts
- Review Greek insurance regulatory framework

**Time Estimate:** 16 hours

**Priority:** **HIGH**

## Quick Reference: Insurance Terms for This RFP

| Term | Definition | RFP Context |
|------|------------|-------------|
| **P&C Underwriting** | Evaluating and pricing property and casualty insurance risks | All CPCU use cases are in Corporate P&C Underwriting |
| **Multi-location Policy** | Insurance policy covering multiple properties/addresses | CPCU1 (Geocoding) processes these |
| **Risk Address** | Physical location of insured property | Geocoded for cat modeling |
| **Catastrophe Model** | Simulation of natural disaster losses | Requires geocoded addresses (CPCU1) |
| **Public Tender** | Government procurement opportunity | CPCU4 monitors for insurance tenders |
| **Underwriting Template** | Standardized format for pricing/quoting | CPCU3 auto-populates these |
| **Policy Contract** | Binding agreement document | CPCU5 generates these |
| **Loss Ratio** | Claims paid / Premiums earned | KPI for underwriting quality |
| **Combined Ratio** | (Losses + Expenses) / Premiums | Overall profitability metric |
| **FNOL** | First Notice of Loss | Initial claims report |

## Integration with Technical Learning

Insurance domain knowledge complements technical skills:
- **IDP** extracts insurance-specific entities (policy numbers, coverage types)
- **RAG** retrieves insurance policy information and guidelines
- **NLP** classifies insurance documents (policies, claims, quotes)
- **Automation** follows insurance business rules and workflows

Learn insurance domain in parallel with IDP/RAG for best results.

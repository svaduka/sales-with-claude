# Sales & Client Representation Capabilities

## Overview
Strong client-facing architect capable of translating complex technical solutions into business value. Acts as a bridge between engineering teams and executive stakeholders.

---

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Client Communication
- Engage with senior client stakeholders
- Present architecture to leadership teams
- Act as single point of contact for solution discussions

---

### 2. Solution Positioning
- Position Databricks Lakehouse as replacement for legacy systems
- Recommend tools (Power BI, Excel integration, Genie AI)
- Align technical solutions with business goals

---

### 3. Requirement Gathering
- Analyze current systems (EBS, Teradata, DEQ)
- Identify gaps and opportunities
- Translate business needs into technical design

---

### 4. Proposal & Strategy
- Define roadmap for:
  - Data migration
  - Reporting modernization
  - Governance implementation
- Suggest scalable and future-proof architectures

---

### 5. Stakeholder Alignment
- Coordinate across:
  - Client teams
  - Internal teams (EPAM)
  - External teams (Azure SCIM, SailPoint)
- Ensure alignment on deliverables and timelines

---

### 6. Conflict Resolution
- Handle ambiguity in requirements
- Resolve ownership gaps (e.g., SCIM, SailPoint dependencies)
- Drive decisions in unclear environments

---

### 7. Documentation & Presentation
- Create:
  - Solution Architecture Documents
  - Mapping documents
  - Implementation plans
- Present to Architecture Review Boards

---

## Business Impact

- Enabled migration from legacy systems to modern data platform
- Improved scalability and performance of reporting systems
- Reduced dependency on manual Excel-based processes
- Established governance and compliance frameworks
